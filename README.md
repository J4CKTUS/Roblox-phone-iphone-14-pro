# Roblox-phone-iphone-14-pro
# 📱 Flowix Phone — iPhone 14 Pro UI for Roblox
A realistic iPhone 14 Pro UI built entirely in Roblox using LocalScript + ScreenGui.
Made by **J4CKTUS** for the Flowix community.
> Join us → **[flowix.gg](https://flowix.gg)** • **[discord.gg/Sf4shXvy](https://discord.gg/Sf4shXvy)**
---
## ✦ Features
- Realistic iPhone 14 Pro look with metallic rim and rounded edges
- Live clock & date on lockscreen
- Tap anywhere to unlock
- Home screen with 12 animated app icons
- Each app opens with a smooth slide animation
- Status bar with signal, wifi and battery icons
- Press **M** to open / close the phone
---
## 🚀 How to use
1. Open your Roblox game in Studio
2. Put the script inside `StarterPlayerScripts` or `StarterCharacterScripts`
3. Hit Play — press **M** in game to open the phone
---
## 📌 Good to know
Look, this isn't perfect. It's a free Roblox script made for fun and the community — don't expect a fully working iPhone.
- The apps are **empty** when you open them, they're just placeholder screens with the app name. No actual functionality inside.
- Some things might look slightly off depending on your screen size or Roblox version.
- It's not pixel-perfect but it's pretty close for what it is.
It's free, it looks cool, and it works. That's the deal.
